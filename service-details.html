Service Details

OpVerse provides an XR-powered platform that transforms medical imaging into interactive 3D experiences. From anatomy learning to VATS port and needle path planning, this help clinicians and trainees plan safer procedures with greater precision and confidence.

What you can expect

Explore patient‑specific 3D models, simulate procedures in XR, and plan safer, more efficient surgeries with an accessible, portable setup.

Immersive 3D VR Anatomy & Surgery Learning

OpVerse provides an immersive VR/AR workspace for medical education and rehearsal. Explore patient‑specific anatomy with true spatial depth, interact with organs and vessels, and train surgical steps inside a virtual OR.

  • Realistic 3D anatomy reconstructed from medical imaging.
  • Interactive manipulation to build spatial intuition.
  • Structured training modules for medical students and residents.

VATS Port Planning

For thoracoscopic or minimally invasive surgery, port placement determines visibility, reach, and ergonomics. Simulate multiple incision strategies on a 3D patient model to select the safest and most effective approach.

  • Compare candidate port sites and instrument angles.
  • Evaluate workspace feasibility before the OR.
  • Improve outcomes by minimizing unnecessary trauma.

Needle Path Planning

Plan dye injection or biopsy trajectories that avoid critical structures. Our XR environment enables intuitive, 3D route design with clear awareness of vessels, bronchi, and ribs.

  • Simulate needle paths with spatial constraints.
  • Reduce risk by avoiding vessels and airways.
  • Increase confidence for preop planning and training.
